<h1 style="font-size: 2em; margin-bottom: 10px;">Evidence-Based Advocacy for Kingston Residents</h1>



<p>As Kingston navigates the multifaceted challenge of climate displacement, this dashboard serves as a bridge between residents and policymakers.</p>



<h3 style="font-size: 1.3em; margin-top: 20px;">Why This Data is Critical for Policy Change</h3>



<ul style="line-height: 1.6;">

&#x20;   <li>During climate disasters, the most vulnerable residents often remain invisible in official recovery statistics.</li>

&#x20;   <li>By aggregating denial reasons, we can pinpoint specific barriers that need mitigation.</li>

&#x20;   <li>We use anonymized practices to protect residents' privacy while longitudinally monitoring the disaster's impact.</li>

</ul>

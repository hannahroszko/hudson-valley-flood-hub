\# Evidence-Based Advocacy for Kingston Residents



As Kingston navigates the multifaceted challenge of climate displacement, this dashboard serves as a bridge between residents and policymakers. 



\### Why This Data is Critical for Policy Change

\*   During climate disasters, the most vulnerable residents often remain invisible in official recovery statistics. This dashboard amplifies their experiences by capturing unmet needs that traditional aid programs might miss.

\*   By aggregating denial reasons—such as ownership verification hurdles for heir’s property—we can pinpoint specific planning and regulatory barriers that need mitigation at the city and county levels.

\*   We use anonymized practices to protect resident privacy while still providing the longitudinal monitoring of disaster impacts necessary for equitable resource allocation and infrastructure prioritization.

\*   Moving beyond anecdotal reports allows local leaders to align city projects with actual community outcomes, fostering the trust and transparency required for long-term climate adaptation in the Hudson Valley.
